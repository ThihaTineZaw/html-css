CREW MANAGEMENT

Recruitment and on-board arrangement

In the selection of new crew members, three managers select crew members transparently and fairly. In addition, trainees are selected through an interview among graduates of Maritime University.

The first stage involves submitting your resume. You can apply by either emailing your resume or submitting it in person at our office .

Your resume has been reviewed, and we believe your skills align well with this role. We'd like to schedule an interview to discuss your experience further.

Upon successful completion of the interview, you will be invited to proceed to a medical checkup to ensure you meet the necessary health requirements for this role.

Following a successful medical examination, you will be considered a potential candidate for employment within our maritime operations.
